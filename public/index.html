About Beachside Rehab

With a holistic approach towards substance abuse and treatment, combined with a conventional 12-step program, every person who visits Beachside Rehab is in position to reclaim their life. It is this unique approach that has allowed us to help many people in your position take back their life. We utilize mind, body, and spiritual elements throughout treatment to enhance the recovery process and fight against the possibility of relapse. We understand that every person is faced with a unique set of circumstances. This is why Beachside Rehab provides specialized treatment for every person who walks through our doors.
